Since 1966, Numex has supplied the scientific community with fine laboratory chemicals and reagents — sourced from the world's most trusted manufacturers, on one uncompromising standard: quality that doesn't vary from shipment to shipment.

What began as a small Mumbai partnership has grown into a full-catalogue distribution house trusted by universities, hospitals, and industrial laboratories across India and abroad. We don't make the chemistry — we make sure the right grade, from the right brand, reaches the right bench, with the paperwork to prove it.

Every consignment is checked against label specification on arrival and packed to survive the onward trip — whether it's going three kilometres across Mumbai or to a lab on another continent.

The purity ladder

One compound, five standards of purity.

The same chemical can serve five very different purposes — a classroom titration and an HPLC assay need different guarantees. We grade every product against the standard the application actually requires.

Grade 01 · LR

Laboratory Reagent

General-purpose grade for routine lab use, qualitative work and teaching laboratories.

Grade 02 · AR

Analytical Reagent

Tighter impurity limits for quantitative analysis, titrations and standard preparation.

Grade 03 · GR

Guaranteed Reagent

Assayed and certified purity for sensitive procedures where trace impurities matter.

Grade 04 · ACS

ACS Grade

Meets American Chemical Society specification limits for research-grade reagents.

Grade 05 · HPLC

HPLC / Spectroscopic

Ultra-high purity solvents for chromatography and instrumentation-grade analysis.
